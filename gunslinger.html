This fall I participated in my first hackathon at HackSC. With a team of 4 we won 1st place for the Myo hack category for our hack called Gunslinger. Not bad for a first timer!
Gunslinger is an online multiplayer game simulating a Wild West duel shootout that you play with a Myo-band, a gesture control armband by Thalmic Labs. Gunslinger uses WebRTC and Socket.io to connect you to other users on the website. Once connected, players wait for the signal to draw. To draw your weapon, you must prime and cock your gun by performing gestures. The first Gunslinger to draw and shoot their weapons wins!
