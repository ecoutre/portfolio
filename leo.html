I began working for Leo beginning last spring and bleeding into the summer. Leo is group chat reinvented. Connect with your favorite people through short-lived photos, videos & texts, captured in the moment. Photos and videos disappear a few seconds after viewing them. Texts last for 24 hours.
App Store Screenshots
At Leo I focused my efforts primarily on product design although I had my hands in just about everything (as it is with most startups). As we continually considered to add new features to the mobile app I created mockups & implemented rapid prototyping followed by conducting user testing. The focus of usability testing was to determine which potential features would enhance the value of the core functionality & which would ultimately detract from it.
Leo is backed by some great VCs including: Battery Ventures, Crunchfund, Freestyle Capital, Greylock Partners, Kleiner Perkins Caufield & Byers, SV Angel & a group of all star angel investors.
