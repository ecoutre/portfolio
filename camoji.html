Starting this past summer I worked with the Camoji team in San Francisco primarily as a product/UX designer. Camoji started as the simplest way to record & text animated GIFs to your friends: tap & hold to record, swipe to send. With Camoji's latest release you can now post to the Camoji Network as well as view, upvote, and comment on your favorite GIFs from the community.
Camoji is backed by some of the best Venture Capitalists in the Silicon Valley including: Battery Ventures, Crunchfund, Freestyle Capital, Greylock Partners, Kleiner Perkins Caufield & Byers, SV Angel & a group of all star angel investors.
At Camoji I was incredibly lucky to be a part of such a small team which allowed me to be involved in every decision relating to the direction of the product and company. This was a big responsibility because ultimately every decision I made would contribute to the success or failure of the product. Conducting user research pushed me to think more creatively about how to approach the product & have a deep understanding of users to be able to create the most value.
My hand in conceiving & building Camoji led to a successful launch. We were featured as Best New Apps on the Apple Appstore & within the first two weeks over one million Camojis had been sent & it rose up to #4 in the photo and video section.
